Antibiotics: Good or Bad?

Are you sure that all of the antibiotics you take necessary?

A lot of us take antibiotics when we're not feeling good, not doubting it's help.
It's one of the things that we do without questionning, something we're used to.

Researches showed that a not-so-small amount of the antibiotics we take are unnecessary/ don't help as much as it's supposed to.
So is it really helping? If it's not, why do people make us think that they do and what's their agenda??
